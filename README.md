Insect pests are known to be a major cause of damage to commercially important agricultural crops. Categorization of insect pests plays a crucial role in agricultural pest forecasting, which is vital for food security. This study aims to classification of the insects in rice, wheat, etc. using different Approaches and insect pest detection algorithms.
